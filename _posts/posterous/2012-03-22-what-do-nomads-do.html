So what to nomads do when they stop moving? ?? The energy that was put forth in constant movement must be re-directed into other endeavors. ?? It is not the cessation of movements, but its redirection. ?? So we build and create.

Building a garden is the first order of business.


Utilizing local and free materials is the name of the game. ??We went to the river and harvested Salt Cedar branches. ??As Salt Cedar is a harmful invasive species, any trimming of it is good to prevent further growth. ?? We cut posts about 1-2 inches thick and 2-3 feet ??long and drove them into the ground. ??Around these we wove the suckers from the Salt Cedar to create raised beds.??


Six inches under the top soil is pure, beautiful clay...perfect for creating an adobe mix of clay, dry leaves and grass to coat the inside of the walls. ??


Here come our first little sprouts.
