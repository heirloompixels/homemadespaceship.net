Lots more natural dyeing going on, check out newtribetextiles.net for more.  
This is raw silk yarn and fabric dyed with Fustic.  Thanks to Wikipedia here is some info on it - 

Maclura tinctoria, commonly known as Old Fustic or Dyer's mulberry is a medium to large tree of the Neotropics, from Mexico to Argentina. It produces a yellow dye called fustic primarily known for coloring khaki fabric for U.S. military apparel during World War I.
