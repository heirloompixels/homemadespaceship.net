The wilderness of the American west.   This is the place where we will roam, in our strange and marvelous caravan, living and creating  under the sky.  Our first deep look into the south-west was early last spring when we walked from the Superstition wilderness (just outside of Phoenix, AZ) to Truth or Consequences, NM.  Walking around 20 miles every day we crossed Sonoran desert valleys, climbed over and through numerous mountain ranges, watching the landscape change beneath our feet.  This is a land of harsh plenty and mystery.
