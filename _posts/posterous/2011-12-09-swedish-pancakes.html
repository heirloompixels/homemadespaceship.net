This is an awesome recipe that was cooked for us recently, and it was AWESOME. ??Just thought I'd share the pancake love!

From the 1945 'Woman's Home Companion Cook Book'....here we go.

Flour, 1/2 cup (Gluten free mix, or all purpose)
Salt, 1 teaspoon
Sugar, teaspoon
Eggs, well beaten, 3
Milk, 3 cups
Butter, melted, 3 Tablespoons

-mix flour, salt and sugar. (since we are no longer in the days of needing to sift flour...I left it out)

-To the beaten eggs add milk and melted butter; pour into flour mixture and stir until combined.??

-Bake on hot greased griddle, using about 1/2 cup batter to make large cakes 8 to 9 inches in diameter.??

-Pile the pancakes like a layer cake on a big plate, pouring syrup (or jelly, honey or what ever suits your fancy) between each layer. ??Cut into pie shaped pieces to serve, and top with more good things.??
